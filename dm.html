Design 4 Concept: Degital Media Page

There is only one page of this design →

This is a page for the Digital Media Class. We choose photos of students in the class taking photographs, creating sketches, and editing on photoshop. The design is pretty simple. We didn't use a lot of additional elements except for 1 camera clipart on the top left corner. We used borders on each of the photo to create the frame. We also cut out 4 people taking photos and put them at the bottom of the page, which makes the page looks more interesting. The text on the bottom right corner records what the class does and how the yearbook is formed.
